Education Requirements:
Majoring in Computer Science is the best way to go and obtaining at least a Bachelor’s degree. Also individuals must be able to stay up-to-date with the latest technology as it evolves.

Description:
Software developers are the people behind the development for applications in day-to-day use or they developer the system that controls the devices. They maintain programs with regular updates and advise users when to update. During the process of development a software developer should be documenting what they are writing for any future peers that may review the code to get a better understanding. Most software developers work as part of a team to deliver a product.
